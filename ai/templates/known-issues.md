# Known Issues

Managed ledger for recurring bugs, regressions, environment quirks, and active workarounds.

## Rules

- Update an existing entry before adding a duplicate.
- Keep entries evidence-backed; separate observations from guesses.
- Do not include secrets, credentials, or raw logs with tokens.
- Close or archive entries after the exit criteria are verified.
